Keto Bread

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
ground psyllium husk powder 1⁄3 cup (1¾ oz.)
almond flour 1¼ cups (5 oz.)

Step 1:Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
Step 2:Mix the dry ingredients in a large bowl. Bring the water to a boil..
Step 3: Add vinegar and egg whites to the dry ingredients, and combine well. Add boiling water, while beating with a hand mixer for about 30 seconds. Don't over mix the dough, the consistency should resemble Play-Doh.
Step 4: Moisten hands with a little olive oil and shape dough into 6 separate rolls. Place on a greased baking sheet. Top with optional sesame seeds.

Keto Egg Muffins

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
scallions, finely chopped 2 (1 oz)
cooked bacon or salami, chopped 5 oz
eggs 12
red or green pesto 2 tbsp
salt and pepper amount to taste
shredded cheddar cheese 1½ cups (6 oz.)

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
Step 2: Line a muffin tin with insertable baking cups, or grease a silicone muffin tin with butter, or use a non-stick muffin tin (two muffins per serving).
Step 3: Add scallions and the cooked bacon or salami, to the bottom of the tin.
Step 4: Whisk together the eggs, pesto, salt, and pepper, until combined.
Step 5: Add the cheese in with the egg mixture and combine well.
Step 6: Pour the egg mixture on top of the scallions and meat.
Step 7: Bake for 15–20 minutes, depending on the size of the muffin tin.

Keto Seed Crackers

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 30
Ingredients Quantity
almond flour 1⁄3 cup (1 1⁄3 oz.)
unsalted sunflower seeds 1⁄3 cup (1 2/3 oz.)
unsalted pumpkin seeds 1⁄3 cup (1 1⁄3 oz.)
flaxseed or chia seeds 1⁄3 cup (2 oz.)
sesame seeds 1⁄3 cup (1 2⁄3 oz.)
ground psyllium husk powder 1 tbsp
salt 1 tsp
melted coconut oil ¼ cup
boiling water 1 cup

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 300°F (150°C).
Step 2: Mix all dry ingredients in a bowl. Add boiling water and oil. Mix together with a wooden fork.
Step 3: Keep working the dough until it forms a ball and has a gel-like consistency.
Step 4: Place the dough on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Add another paper on top and use a rolling pin to flatten the dough evenly.
Step 5: Remove the upper paper and bake on the lower rack for about 40-45 minutes, check occasionally. Seeds are heat sensitive so pay close attention towards the end.
Step 6: Turn off the oven and leave the crackers to dry in the oven. Once dried and cool, break into pieces and spread a generous amount of butter on top.

Source: Diet Doctor Keto Seed Crackers
